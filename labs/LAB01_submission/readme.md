# The Legend of Kraken's Cove

## Story
You are a young buccaneer who has just arrived at Port Royal with nothing but a dream of becoming the most famous pirate in the Caribbean. You've heard whispers of Captain Blackbeard's legendary treasure, the Golden Kraken Idol, hidden somewhere in the mysterious Kraken's Cove. Your journey will take you across the high seas, through Siren's Reef, and eventually to the dark Skull Island. Can you defeat the ghost of Blackbeard and claim the treasure?

## How to Play

### Running the Game
```bash
python game.py
```

### Commands
- go [direction] (e.g., go west): Move between locations.
- look: Re-examine your current location.
- fight: Attack an enemy present in your location.
- take [item]: Pick up an item from the current location.
- use [item]: Use an item from your inventory (e.g., use rum).
- inventory or i: See what items you're carrying.
- help: See the list of commands.
- quit: Exit the game.

## Goal
Defeat Captain Blackbeard in his cave, claim the Golden Kraken Idol, and reach the Hidden Cove to win!

## Tips
- Keep an eye on your health (HP).
- Use items like Rum or Grog to restore health during or between fights.
- Be prepared for tough enemies on Skull Island and in the Cave.
- Sometimes running is the better option if you're low on health.
